    Snickerdoodle Cookies

  • Prep Time: 20 minutes
  • Cook Time: 10 minutes
  • Total Time: 30 minutes
  • Servings: 48 cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups white sugar
  • ½ cup butter, softened
  • ½ cup shortening
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoon cream of tartar
  • 1 teaspoon Baking Soda
  • ¼ teaspoon Salt

    • Cinnamon-Sugar Coating

  • 2 tablespoons white Sugar
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Beat sugar, butter, shortening, eggs, and vanilla in a large bowl until smooth and creamy.
  3. Whisk flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, and salt together in a separate bowl. Gradually mix dry ingredients mixture into the wet ingredients just until combined.
  4. Shape dough into walnut-sized balls.
  5. Make cinnamon-sugar: Combine sugar and cinnamon in a small bowl or zip-top plastic bag.
  6. Place dough balls in cinnamon-sugar and roll or shake until coated. Place 2 inches apart on ungreased baking sheets.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven until set but not too hard, 8 to 10 minutes.
  8. Remove from the oven and immediately transfer to wire racks to cool.
