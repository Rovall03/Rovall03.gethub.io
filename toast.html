  • French Toast
  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Cook Time: 10 minutes
  • total Time: 20 minutes
  • Servings: 8

Ingredients

  • 4 Eggs
  • ⅔ Cup of Milk
  • ⅓ Cup of Flour
  • ⅓ Cup of Sugar
  • Pinch of Salt
  • 1 teaspoon of Vanilla
  • ½ tsp of Cinnamon
  • 6 - 8 Slices of Texas Toast
  • 4 Tablespoons of Butter

Steps

  1. Whisk the following ingredients: eggs, milk, salt, cinnamon, flour, sugar, vanilla.
  2. Melt Butter in a skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add eggs, one at a time, mix.
  4. Soak Texas Toast each side for 30 seconds
  5. Once soaked place on skillet and cook each side - takes a couple minutes per side
  6. Serve with hot syrup
